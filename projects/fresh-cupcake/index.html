Where were the first cupcakes made?

Ancient Rome is considered the birthplace of cupcakes. Then they were made from coarse barley flour mixed with raisins, nuts and pomegranate seeds, and bee honey was a pleasant accent. Such luxury was allowed to be served only to the most privileged Roman persons.
